## 🌟 What is Plagiarism-Detection-System?

Plagiarism-Detection-System is a web-based application that lets you check if two or more text documents share significant similarity. It helps spot copied or closely matching text quickly. This system combines a simple website to upload and view your text submissions with a smart backend tool that compares the content for plagiarism signs.

You do not need any technical background to use this. Just upload or paste your texts and get your results through a clean, easy-to-use interface.

---

## 🎯 Why Use This System?

Plagiarism is a big concern in schools, colleges, and workplaces where original content matters. Manual checking can take hours and still miss copied parts. This system automates the process, making it faster and more reliable. It can be used by students, teachers, editors, or anyone who wants to check the originality of text quickly.

## ⚙️ Key Features

- **Web Interface:** Upload or paste your text easily from any modern web browser.
- **Text Comparison:** The system compares your texts and highlights similarity.

- **Clear Results:** Shows similarity scores and matching parts clearly.
- **Secure Processing:** Your texts stay private and are processed only during your session.
